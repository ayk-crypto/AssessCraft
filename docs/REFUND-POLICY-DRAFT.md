# AssessCraft refund policy — counsel-review draft

AssessCraft offers a **7-day satisfaction period** beginning on the original purchase date, subject to applicable consumer law.

To request a refund, the customer must contact support within the stated period using the purchase email and order reference. Onset Media may ask for reasonable information needed to identify the order and understand a technical issue, but will not require access credentials or respondent data.

Approved refunds are returned through the original payment method. Processing times depend on the payment provider. A refunded license may be cancelled and access to commercial downloads, updates, and priority support may end.

The policy should state how renewals, upgrades, taxes, duplicate purchases, chargebacks, custom services, and statutory consumer rights are treated. Final wording must match the actual Freemius checkout configuration and receive legal review.
